About Randal Hospital

Randal hospital is a world class hospital located along Mombasa Road.It was started in the year 2016 and it has gradually grown to support all kinds of patients.We offer both inpatient and outpatient services.

Mission

To deliver timely and compassionate medical services to our patients and their families to the highest possible standard.

Vision

To be a leading healthcare provider in East and Central Africa and to benchmark itself against the highest attainable world standards, seeking continuous improvement.

Services offered

Obsetrics and Gynecology-Care for women during pregnancy and childbirth.

Pediatrics-Care for children and their diseases

Urology-Deals with function and disorders of the urinary system

Dermatology-Deals with diagnosis and treatment of skin disorders

Neurology-deals with the nerves and the nervous system

Orthopaedic-Deals with deformities of bones or muscles

Eye clinic-Cares for the eyes

On every Friday we offer free cancer screening
